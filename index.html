About Us

Mercury Products was established in 2005 and is currently a leading manufacturer of home and personal care products. Mercury have been in the market for 15 years and produce a range of products that include candle, cosmetics and most recently diversified into the furniture industry making doors.

From the outset Mercury Products was focused on the unique needs and opportunities in Malawi.

Today Mercury Products is a major manufacturing and marketing enterprise with specialised expertise:

  • 120 employees across 3 divisions.
  • Manufacturing facilities for Beauty Care products, Candles and doors.
  • 20 products including leading brands in Malawi – LISA, Try Me and Mercury.
  • 2Sold throughout Malawi in the Southern, Central, Northern regions and Neighbouring Countries.

Vision

To provide quality and affordable home and personal care products to the community whilst becoming a market leader in the region.

Our Values

We run our business in an ethical manner, dealing fairly and respectfully with our consumers, customers, suppliers, employees, environment and the broader community in which we operate.

We strive to always maintain a healthy balance between entrepreneurialism and professionalism.

Decisions should be guided by what is in the company’s best interests over the long term, rather than the pursuit of unsustainable short-term benefits.
