About Us

Mercury Products was established in 2005 and is currently a leading manufacturer of home and personal care products. Mercury have been in the market for 15 years and produce a range of products that include candle, cosmetics and most recently diversified into the furniture industry making doors.

Vision

To provide quality and affordable home and personal care products to the community whilst becoming a market leader in the region.
